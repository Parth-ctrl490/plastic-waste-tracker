Our Mission & Goals

Revolutionizing ocean conservation through AI-powered plastic detection technology

Our Mission

To revolutionize marine conservation by developing cutting-edge AI technology that detects, maps, and monitors plastic pollution in our oceans. We aim to create a comprehensive satellite-based system that provides real-time environmental intelligence to researchers, policymakers, and conservationists worldwide.

Through our innovative approach combining NASA satellite imagery with machine learning, we're building the future of ocean monitoring - one pixel at a time.

Strategic Goals

1

Advanced Detection System

Develop a state-of-the-art AI model capable of identifying plastic debris in marine environments with 95%+ accuracy using satellite imagery.

  • Multi-spectral image analysis
  • Deep learning object detection
  • Real-time processing capabilities
  • Cloud-based infrastructure
2

Global Mapping Platform

Create an interactive web platform that visualizes plastic pollution hotspots and trends across rivers, lakes, and coastal areas worldwide.

  • Interactive pollution heat maps
  • Historical trend analysis
  • Mobile-responsive design
  • Open-source data API
3

Scientific Collaboration

Partner with marine research institutions and environmental organizations to validate our findings and expand the dataset.

  • Academic research partnerships
  • Peer-reviewed publications
  • Open data sharing protocols
  • Citizen science integration
4

Policy Impact

Provide actionable insights to governments and NGOs to inform evidence-based environmental policies and cleanup initiatives.

  • Policy recommendation reports
  • Stakeholder engagement
  • Impact assessment tools
  • International collaboration
